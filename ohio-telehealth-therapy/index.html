Online Therapy in Ohio

Virtual Therapy for Ohio Residents

Inner World Counseling Services provides 100% virtual therapy to adults throughout Ohio. Whether you're in Columbus, Cleveland, Cincinnati, Dayton, or anywhere else in the state, you can access evidence-based therapy from the comfort and privacy of your own home.

Why Choose Virtual Therapy in Ohio?

Finding a therapist who specializes in trauma and uses evidence-based methods like EMDR or CPT can be difficult in many parts of Ohio. Telehealth removes geographic barriers so you can work with a specialist regardless of where you live in the state.

Services Available to Ohio Clients

  • Anxiety Therapy — Break free from the cycle of worry and hypervigilance
  • Trauma Therapy — Process past experiences that still drive your behavior today
  • EMDR Therapy — Reprocess traumatic memories to reduce their emotional charge
  • Cognitive Processing Therapy — Challenge stuck points and unhelpful beliefs
  • Addiction Counseling — Address the root causes behind substance use
  • People-Pleasing & Fawn Response — Stop over-functioning and start living for yourself

About Your Therapist

Deirdre Kuvaas, LPCC, is a Licensed Professional Clinical Counselor with specialized training in trauma and addiction. She holds active licensure in Ohio and is passionate about helping high-functioning adults who are tired of just "getting by."
