Online Therapy in Ohio

Virtual Therapy for Ohio Residents

Ohio's major metros — Columbus, Cleveland, Cincinnati, Dayton — are full of high-achieving professionals managing demanding careers while quietly carrying anxiety, burnout, or unresolved trauma. Access to a therapist who specializes in evidence-based trauma treatment like EMDR or CPT, rather than general talk therapy, can be difficult to find even in larger cities. Inner World Counseling Services provides 100% virtual trauma therapy to adults across Ohio — no commute, no waiting room, same clinical outcomes.

Why Ohio Clients Choose Telehealth Trauma Therapy

Telehealth removes every logistical barrier between you and specialized care. Whether you are in Columbus navigating a high-pressure career or in a smaller Ohio community with limited local options, you can access the same evidence-based EMDR and CPT treatment from your home, office, or anywhere private.

Services Available to Ohio Clients

  • Anxiety Therapy — Break free from the cycle of worry and hypervigilance
  • Trauma Therapy — Process past experiences that still drive your behavior today
  • EMDR Therapy — Reprocess traumatic memories to reduce their emotional charge
  • Cognitive Processing Therapy — Challenge stuck points and unhelpful beliefs
  • Addiction Counseling — Address the root causes behind substance use
  • People-Pleasing & Fawn Response — Stop over-functioning and start living for yourself

Frequently Asked Questions — Ohio

Do I need to be in Ohio during our sessions?

Yes. Ohio telehealth licensure requires that you are physically located in Ohio at the time of each session. If you are traveling, please let us know in advance so we can adjust your schedule.

Is EMDR available to Ohio residents via telehealth?

Yes. Online EMDR is just as effective as in-person EMDR and is fully available to Ohio residents. Sessions use a secure video platform with a digital bilateral stimulation tool — no special equipment needed on your end.
