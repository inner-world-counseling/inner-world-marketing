Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer virtual therapy in my state?

I am licensed for telehealth in ND, MN, OH, and UT.

What is the focus of your practice?

I specialize in evidence-based trauma recovery and anxiety treatment for high-functioning adults.

Is EMDR effective for PTSD when done via telehealth?

Yes, online EMDR is highly effective for treating PTSD and yields similar results to in-person therapy. We use a secure virtual platform equipped with visual or auditory bilateral stimulation.

What is the difference between EMDR or CPT for trauma?

EMDR utilizes bilateral stimulation to help your brain process and desensitize traumatic memories. CPT is a cognitive-behavioral approach that helps you identify, challenge, and modify unhelpful beliefs and thoughts related to the trauma.

Do you provide superbills for out-of-network insurance?

Yes, I can provide a detailed superbill that you can submit to your insurance provider for potential out-of-network reimbursement.

How long does a typical trauma therapy session last?

A typical trauma therapy session lasts between 50 to 55 minutes, providing sufficient time for deep processing and grounding before the session ends.

What is EMDR Therapy?
EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing) is a structured therapy that encourages the patient to briefly focus on the trauma memory while simultaneously experiencing bilateral stimulation, which is associated with a reduction in the vividness and emotion associated with the trauma memories.
What is CPT Therapy?
Cognitive Processing Therapy (CPT) is a specific type of cognitive behavioral therapy that helps patients learn how to modify and challenge unhelpful beliefs related to trauma.
What is High-Functioning Anxiety?
A term used to describe individuals who experience significant internal anxiety and perfectionism while maintaining a high-achieving, successful outward appearance.
What is Trauma-Informed Telehealth?
Trauma-informed telehealth is a specialized approach to online therapy that prioritizes safety, choice, and empowerment, ensuring that the virtual environment supports deep healing for PTSD and attachment wounds.
How does Online EMDR Therapy work?
Online EMDR therapy utilizes secure video platforms and digital bilateral stimulation tools to provide the same evidence-based trauma processing as in-person sessions, allowing for healing from the comfort of your own home.
